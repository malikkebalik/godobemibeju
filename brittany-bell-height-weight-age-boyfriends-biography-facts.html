Brittany Bell is an American model, dancer, beauty pageant titleholder, actor, and television personality who was crowned Miss Arizona USA 2010 and then represented Arizona in Miss USA 2010. She was crowned Miss Guam Universe 2014 and competed at Miss Universe 2014, representing Guam. Moreover, Brittany Bell has also been a part of shows and films such as The Last Movie Star, Taylor’d Problems, Good Cop Bike Cop, Bromance & Betrayal, and Strictly Stalking.
