Netflix Documentary Riles Meghan Markles Half-Siblings

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Meghan Markle’s half-siblings have responded to the Duke and Duchess of Sussex’s recent Netflix docuseries, criticizing it as “horrible” and “hurtful” towards their family.

Speaking on Talk TV, Thomas Markle Jr. expressed his concerns about the documentary, calling it “disturbing” and dismissing its claims as “ridiculous.”

In the docuseries, Prince Harry and Meghan discussed the events leading up to their royal wedding, including the breakdown of Meghan’s relationship with her father, Thomas Markle, 78.

Prince Harry acknowledged feeling responsible for the rift, stating that if Meghan wasn’t with him, her father would still be a part of her life.

Reacting to these remarks, both of Meghan’s half-siblings, Thomas Markle Jr. and Samantha, voiced their offense at the portrayal of their father.

Thomas Markle Jr. described the documentary as “horrible” and “disturbing,” emphasizing that it misrepresented their family and accused Meghan of lying.

He announced plans to work on his own documentary to present their side of the story, claiming it would shed light on the situation with unprecedented files, photos, and documentation.

Samantha Markle also addressed claims made in the documentary, denying accusations that she was the reason her daughter wasn’t invited to the royal wedding.

Thomas Markle Jr. criticized the couple for not inviting their family to the wedding, expressing disbelief at Meghan’s actions and her attempt to change a centuries-old monarchy to her liking.

He also mentioned that Meghan has not been in touch with their father since his stroke.
