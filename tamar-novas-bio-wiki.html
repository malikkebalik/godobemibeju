Who is Tamar Novas?

In 2023, he was seen in the role of Nico in the survival thriller movie Nowhere. This film has got a fair response from the audience as it has ratings of 6.7 on IMDb.

His upcoming screen project Justicia Artificial is in the post-production stage and it will be released soon. Due to his amazing acting in The Sea Inside, he won the Best New Actor Award at the 19th Goya Awards and the 14th Actors and Actresses Union Awards in the year 2005.
