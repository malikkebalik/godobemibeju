Nicolette Robinson and her husband Leslie Odom Jr. are parents for the second time!

The couple welcomed their bundle of joy Able Phineas on 25th March 2021. The happy mom-dad announced the birth news on 1st April 2021 through Instagram.

The Love in the Time of Corona couple is enthusiastic about the new chapter of their life.

Married life of Leslie Odom Jr. and Nicolette Robinson

Leslie Odom Jr. and Nicolette Robinson met each other in the year 2008 when she was auditioning for a production he was the assistant director at. Nicolette was still in college at UCLA. It was his job to make her work and it played well for making them close. Even though the show ended, they remained together forever.

They married on 1st December 2012 and became parents on 23rd April 2017 by welcoming a daughter named Lucille Ruby. She revealed,

“As little kids, we grew up in our separate households across the country from one another, watching the Tony Awards and dreaming that some day we would be a part of that world. I sit here the morning of Tony day, as you are nominated for Best Lead Actor in a Musical,”

The couple has an important deal to have a little bit of romance every single day. They take each other out for a nice dinner and the rule has worked best for saving their marriage.

How much is the net worth of Leslie Odom Jr. in 2021?

Leslie Odom Jr. has gathered a huge net worth of $10 million through his movies and TV work. He has two Oscar nominations for One Night in Miami. Leslie has appeared in multiple films like Red Tails, Murder on the Orient Express, Only, Harriet, Hamilton, One Night in Miami…, Music, The Many Saints of Newark, and Needle in a Time stack.

He is also known for his TV series works like CSI: Miami, The Big House, Threshold, Gilmore Girls, Vanished, Big Day, Smash, Person of Interest, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, One Dollar, Central Park, and Love in the Time of Corona. 

Leslie is also fond of theatre works and loves performing on stage. He has performed in many Broadway and regional theatres. His last most-noted performance was Hamilton for which he was he was nominated for Tony Award for Best Actor in a Musical.

Odom Jr. is also a musician who has four albums to date. His studio albums are The Christmas Album, Mr, Simply Christmas, and Leslie Odom Jr.
