Chrisean Rock Hints That Shes Pregnant Again: One On The Way

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Chrisean Rock has hinted that she may be pregnant again, less than two months after giving birth to her first child.

The bombshell arrived in a conversation with Fashion Nova published on Thursday (October 26), where the reality star was asked if she’d like to have more children.

Chrisean Rock welcomed her first child with Blueface in early September, streaming the birth on Instagram Live while she was surrounded by friends and family – except Blue. The “Thotiana” rapper was instead spending time with his other children’s mother Jaidyn Alexis when Chrisean Jr. was born, and recently asked for her hand in marriage.
