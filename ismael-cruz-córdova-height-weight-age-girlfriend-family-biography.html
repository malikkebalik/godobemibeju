Ismael Cruz Córdova is a Puerto Rican actor, voice actor, and entrepreneur best known for his role as Mando in the children’s animated educational show, Sesame Street. He grew up in Aguas Buenas, Puerto Rico with his siblings and later moved to New York to pursue his dreams. He has worked in a variety of shows and movies like In the Blood, The Good Wife, El Cuerpo del Delito, Ray Donovan, Mary Queen of Scots, White Alligator, Miss Bala, La Loteria, and Berlin Station. Ismael also enjoys a fan base of more than 100k followers on Instagram.
