SF9 Delights Fans with Exciting Winter Comeback Announcement

Publish date: 2024-07-20

The much-anticipated return of SF9 is almost upon them! On December 5 SF9’s 13th mini album will be released on January 8, 2019, marking their comeback next year. A representative from FNC Entertainment has confirmed, “SF9 will be making a comeback with a mini album on January 8.” in reaction to the report.

Take a look at below tweet:

[Breaking News] SF9 to make a comeback on 8th January next year with their 13th Mini Album. #SF9 #에스에프나인 https://t.co/39jsPutXG8 pic.twitter.com/PUiuqcwW21

— Holy Lime (@SF9__Unofficial) December 5, 2023

Since Jaeyoon is currently serving in the military, seven members will be taking part in the upcoming comeback. Since the release of “Puzzle” in January of this year, which was about a year ago, this will be the first new music from the group.

The news that SF9 will carry on as eight members, with Rowoon concentrating on solo projects moving forward, was announced back in September. Rowoon wrote a touching letter to fans after the news broke and announced that he was pulling the group out of any further engagements. Hold on tight for further details regarding the return!
