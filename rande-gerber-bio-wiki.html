Who is Rande Gerber?

Rande Gerber is an American businessman well-known as the owner of the Midnight Oil bar and restaurant chain. Moreover, he is also the founder of the Gerber Group.

He is also famous as the husband of the American model and actress, Cindy Crawford.

Rande Gerber: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Childhood, Ethnicity

Rande Gerber was born in Long Island, New York, USA, on April 27, 1962. As of 2022, his age is 60. He was born to Ellen Peckman (mother) and Jordan Gerber (father).

Both of his parents were born to Jewish families based in New York. The couple moved there from Austria, Poland, Russia, and Belarus.

Gerber spent his childhood days in Hewlett, Long Island, New York, US. His ethnicity is Ashkenazi Jewish.

In which university did Rande Gerber attend?

Talking about his educational background, he attended the University of Arizona. From there, he graduated with a degree in marketing.

Rande Gerber: Professional Life, Career

Rande Gerber is a well-known multimillionaire. Prior to being the businessman and entrepreneur, he is today, he was a successful male model and appeared in several magazines.

Gerber founded the Gerber Group in 2014 and opened its first venue, the bar ‘The Whiskey’ in the Paramount Hotel in New York City in the year 1991. The bar became hugely successful which encouraged him to expand his business more. In 2013, he partnered with his long-term friend, George Clooney, and launched Casamigos Tequila.

In June 2017, they sold it to Diageo plc famous for $700 million making a huge profit. Moreover, the deal also included an additional $300 million possible depending on the company’s performance over the next decade.

His business empire kept growing as in January 2019, it was announced that he, along with his wife Cindy Crawford, Jeff Shell, Mike Meldman, and Jay Sures would purchase the Hollywood deli, Nate ‘n Al.

Gerber’s wife, Cindy Crawford is a famous American model and actress. She became so successful in modeling that it made her an international celebrity leading to a number of roles in films and televisions.

During the 80s and 90s, Cindy was among the most popular supermodels in the world and had a huge presence on magazine covers, runways, and fashion campaigns.
