In January of 2006, Kimberley joined The Post-Standard as the contributing sports writer. Six months after that, the sports journo joined The Record as a Local Sports Intern. After 10 months of internship, she became the sports reporter for The Record.

Starting November of 2007, Kimberley Martin joined Newsday Media Group where she covered NFL, MLB, NBA, and NHL. After four and a half years, she became the New York Jets Beat Writer. She worked for Newsday Media Group for another five and a half years before she quit her job in August of 2017.
