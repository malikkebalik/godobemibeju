Matt Le Tissier is a former professional footballer who has represented England on eight occasions. He played professionally for Southampton before moving to non-league football in 2002. On the field, he played as an attacking midfielder. He is Southampton’s all-time leading scorer, trailing only Mick Channon, and was named PFA Young Player of the Year in 1990.
