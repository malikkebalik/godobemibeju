Who is Aubrey Frances?

Aubrey Frances is an American child actress. She is best known for her role as Lily Tucker–Pritchett on the American television mockumentary sitcom, Modern Family.

She is the youngest star on the red carpet at the 2012 and 2013 Primetime Emmy Awards.
