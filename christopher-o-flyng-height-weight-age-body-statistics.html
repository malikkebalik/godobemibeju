    Christopher O’Flyng Facts

  • Christopher was inspired to begin his journey on YouTube after witnessing the rise and success of Ryan Higa and the comedy group named Smosh.
  • He convinced his friend Luke Korn to participate in his endeavor and together they were channel partners at UncleKornicob for more than 3 years starting in 2011. After their split, Christopher began his self-titled channel.
  • Apart from his regular videos on YouTube, Christopher is also well-known for posting content on youth-based issues and has openly discussed subjects such as homos*xuality, drug abuse, and unsafe daredevil stunts.
  • He has also been involved with several charitable organizations, notably WhyHunger, a non-profit organization that works to provide meals to underprivileged children. To raise funds for the organization, he had even offered his fans a free trip and a coffee date.
  • His stated dream is to be a filmmaker and he has worked on developing quite a few stories of his own.
  • Christopher’s popularity has seen him receive acting offers as well such as the lead role in the horror film titled Soul Reaper.
