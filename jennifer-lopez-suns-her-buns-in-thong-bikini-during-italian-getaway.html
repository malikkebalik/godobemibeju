Jennifer Lopez suns her buns in thong bikini during Italian getaway

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Suns out, buns out.

Jennifer Lopez put on a cheeky display in a tiny white bikini as she enjoyed a sunny day on Italy’s Amalfi Coast Thursday, with the “Let’s Get Loud” singer sporting a string swimsuit to shoot a promotional post for her Delola cocktail brand on Instagram.

Lopez, 54, relaxed on a striped lounge chair as she soaked up some rays by the sea, wearing a classic white bikini from Swim by Di featuring a thong bottom that showed off plenty of booty.

The “Shotgun Wedding” star brought some glam in the form of her signature gold hoop earrings and aviator sunglasses with her bathing suit, adding a thick cuff bracelet and two woven styles on the other arm.

She covered her hair with a colorful silk scarf, adding a straw hat with a green ribbon around the brim for her Delola photoshoot.

“Back in my element,” she captioned her Instagram post, tagging photographer Ana Carballosa.

In the pics, a more glammed-up J.Lo beams as she poses in the same striped chair, adding a silver bucket filled with various flavors of her premixed cocktails and a tray of fresh fruit.

This is the second time this month the “Marry Me” star — who previously credited her ageless looks, in part, to cutting out alcohol — has shared a post promoting her new liquor brand.

On August 1, Lopez stepped outside of her stunning Hamptons home to reveal a Delola drink cart while wearing a summery mint green floral dress — and fans can grab a similar style on Amazon for under $20.

As for her white-hot bikini, we’ll cheers to that.
