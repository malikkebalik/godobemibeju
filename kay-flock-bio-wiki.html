Who is Kay Flock?

Kay Flock is an American Rapper, Actor, and Composer. Kay is best known for his work on the Kay Flock Feat Shake It (Music) in 2022, Cardi B, Dougie B, and Bory300, Kay Flock Feat. Make a Movie (Music) in 2022, Fivio Foreign, and Kay Flock Feat. G Herbo Being Honest (Remix) in 2021.

Moreover, Flock was also seen as a featured artist in Lil Tjay’s Not in the Mood, a Non-Single album in 2021, alongside Fivio Foreign.

On the other hand, Kay Flock holds American nationality and belongs to multiracial ethnicity. Moving toward his educational background, Perez attended Local School relocated in the Bronx.

Career and Professional Life

Kay Flock initiated his music career in May 2020 with the release of his first single, “FTO”; subsequent singles included “Opp Spotter” (featuring B-Lovee), “Brotherly Love” (featuring B-Lovee and Dougie B), and “PSA.”

On November 5, 2021, he released his debut mixtape, The D.O.A. Tape, which reached the top of the Heatseekers Albums chart.

He later released “Being Honest,” which samples XXXTentacion’s “Changes.”

Moreover, Kay Flock released “Shake It” with Dougie B and Cardi B, featuring Bory300, in April 2022 which also reached number 51 on the Billboard Hot 100.
