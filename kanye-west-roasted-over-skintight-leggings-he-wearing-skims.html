Kanye West roasted over skintight leggings: 'He wearing Skims?'

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Fans are not saying “yay” about Ye’s latest look.

Kanye West is getting trolled on Twitter for stepping out in a pair of skintight black leggings, with many comparing the style to something his ex-wife, Kim Kardashian, would sell as part of her Skims shapewear line.

The controversial rapper was snapped heading to his warehouse in Los Angeles in the tights, which he paired with a Manchester United football jersey and a set of his Yeezy brand’s Not Too Sole-ful shoes.

“He wearing SKIMS?” one social media user asked.

“Not Yeggings,” another cracked.

Ye’s second-skin pants and sock boots appear to be part of his Yeezy Season 10 drop, which he unveiled during a secret fashion show in LA Monday.

“Is Kanye taking up ballet,” a commenter asked of the star’s tights.

“It’s giving scuba flipper realness,” another joked of his look.

A third shared an image of someone washing their eyeballs, adding, “This how I feel after seeing Kanye west in his new leggings.”
