Overall Meaning

The Bear Cha-cha-cha song from the children's TV show Bear in the Big Blue House is an energetic and catchy tune that encourages children to dance along. The lyrics talk about a feeling that the singer gets, which is an itching, twitching, scritching, and scratching. This sensation is the signal to do the Bear Cha-cha-cha dance. The lyrics then describe how different parts of the body respond to this feeling, like toes starting to twitch and arms and legs getting the itch. The chorus line of the song repeatedly urges listeners to “Cha-cha-cha” and join in the dance.


The song encourages audience participation and motivation. In addition, the lyrics may also connect with children who experience restless, active behavior and provide a channel for them to release their energy. It also implicitly teaches children how to recognize physical signals and how to respond positively to them.


Overall, the lyrics of this song are simple, repetitive and easy to remember. It combines a positive and cheerful tone with active body movements, providing an entertaining way for children to learn about rhythm.
