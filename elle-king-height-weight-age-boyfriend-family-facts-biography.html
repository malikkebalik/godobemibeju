Elle King is an American singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, television personality, voice actor, and actor. She has toured with several artists including Of Monsters and Men, Train, James Bay, The Chicks, Heart, Joan Jett, Michael Kiwanuka, and Miranda Lambert. Elle has released many albums, tracks, and extended plays such as Love Stuff, Shake the Spirit, In Isolation, Ex’s & Oh’s, America’s Sweetheart, Good Girls, Please Come Home for Christmas, and Under the Influence. Moreover, she has also been cast in shows and movies like Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo, Wild Cherry, Pete the Cat, The Benchwarmers, The Last Playlist, Say Yes to The Dress, and Love, Weddings & Other Disasters.
