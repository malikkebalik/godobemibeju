The Meaning Behind The Song: Build a Boat (Remix) by Colton Dixon

Publish date: 2024-07-07
TitleBuild a Boat (Remix)
ArtistColton Dixon featuring Gabby Barrett
Writer/ComposerSeth Mosley, Sandro Cavazza, Mikey Gormley, Johan Lindbrandt & Colton Dixon
AlbumCanvas (2023)
Release DateJanuary 27, 2023
GenrePop, Christian, Christian Pop

Build a Boat (Remix) is a captivating and inspirational song performed by Colton Dixon and featuring Gabby Barrett. Released on January 27, 2023, as part of his album “Canvas,” the song is infused with elements of pop and Christian pop genres.

The lyrics of “Build a Boat (Remix)” convey a powerful message of unwavering faith and resilience in the face of adversity. The opening lines – “I’ve been wanderin’ through the desert, ain’t seen a cloud in forever over me” – depict a sense of spiritual desolation and longing for relief.

However, the songwriter, Colton Dixon, emphasizes his belief in the imminent arrival of rain, symbolizing hope, rejuvenation, and a fresh start. He believes in the transformative power of God, who can bring life to dry bones and illuminate with a guiding light.

This song resonates with people who have experienced moments of uncertainty and doubt, where the future seems bleak and hope feels out of reach. It serves as a reminder to hold onto faith and trust in a higher power that will lead us to the promised land.

Personally, this song holds a special place in my heart because it mirrors my own journey of faith and resilience. There have been times in my life when I felt lost, wandering through my own deserts, facing challenges that seemed insurmountable. In those moments, it was my unwavering belief and trust in something greater than myself that kept me going.

Listening to “Build a Boat (Remix)” serves as a constant reminder of the power of faith and the presence of a guiding force in our lives. It encourages me to build a metaphorical boat in the desert places of life, preparing for the storms that may come and knowing that with the wind in my sails, I can navigate through any challenge.

One of the standout lines in the chorus, “I’ll ride the storm ’cause I got You by my side,” encapsulates the essence of the song. It showcases the unwavering belief that even in the midst of the storm, we are not alone. We have a source of strength and love that will never fail or fade.

Through the collaboration between Colton Dixon and Gabby Barrett, the song becomes even more powerful. Their voices blend harmoniously, amplifying the message of hope and strength.

The bridge of the song, “I’ll build a boat, so let it rain,” is a call to action, a declaration of readiness to face whatever challenges may come our way. It encourages us to embrace the storms, knowing that they can serve as catalysts for growth and transformation.

“Build a Boat (Remix)” is a testament to the power of music to touch our souls and uplift our spirits. It reminds us that even in the darkest times, there is always a glimmer of hope and a way forward.

In conclusion, Colton Dixon’s “Build a Boat (Remix)” is a beautiful song that speaks to the power of faith and resilience. It invites us to build our boats in the desert places, preparing for the storms of life while trusting in the unwavering love of a higher power. This song has touched the hearts of many, including myself, and serves as a powerful reminder that we are never alone on this journey.
