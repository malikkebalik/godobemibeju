All About Thomas Brown Wife

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Thomas Brown is one lucky man to have such a supportive wife who has stuck with him through every success and challenge.

He and his wife, Jessica, are parents to three sons: Orlando, Tyson, and Judah.

Thomas Brown, born on May 15, 1986, currently serves as the offensive coordinator for the Carolina Panthers in the NFL.

He commenced his coaching journey in 2011 as a strength and conditioning coach for Georgia.

Since then, Brown has worked with various teams, including Chattanooga, Marshall, Wisconsin, Miami, and South Carolina.

On February 12, 2020, Brown joined the Los Angeles Rams coaching staff as the running backs coach. His role expanded when he was appointed assistant head coach on February 23, 2021.

The team won the Super Bowl LVI when they defeated the Cincinnati Bengals 23-20.

Thomas Brown Wife, Jessica Brown

Thomas Brown, the offensive coordinator for the Carolina Panthers, exchanged wedding vows with his wife, Jessica.

The couple are the parents of three sons named Orlando, Tyson, and Judah.

Thomas Brown’s growth as a communicator is often attributed to his relationship with Jessica, described by his parents as a “gregarious” woman.

According to his mother, Louise, Jessica’s natural encouragement played a pivotal role in fostering Thomas’s verbal expression.

Reflecting on his coaching style, Thomas acknowledges that the energy he brings directly reflects the communicative work he has invested in.

He specifically credits Jessica for influencing him to enhance his verbal skills, especially given her outgoing and social nature.

Aside from being a supportive spouse, Jessica Brown is the founder and owner of Anchored in Faith, a venture she initiated to inspire others to strengthen their relationship with God.

Anchored in Faith offers a range of tools, including books, prayer journals, devotionals, and a faith-based daily planner, all designed to foster a deeper connection with spirituality.

Originally hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, Jessica currently resides in California with her husband, Thomas, and their three boys.

His Wife Sticks Up For Him

The Carolina Panthers find themselves in a familiar slump, with another loss marking a 1-12 record. The New Orleans Saints handed them a 28-6 defeat, but the real drama unfolded off the field.

However, the true spectacle unfolded away from the playing field.

Following a lackluster performance from the first overall pick, Bryce Young, and the Panthers’ offense, a user on X (formerly Twitter) expressed frustration.

He wrote, “If we’re keeping it a buck… Thomas Brown should be fired tomorrow.”

In response to the fan’s critique, Brown’s wife, Jessica A. Brown, stepped into the conversation. She reassured the fan that her husband’s departure was imminent.

“Ummm, we are fired!” she retorted. “We just have to stay behind because someone has to call the plays.”

Jessica A. Brown took a bold stance, challenging anyone interested in taking over her husband’s role to “give Tepper a holler,” emphasizing the less-than-pleasant nature of the situation as if this is FUN for us.”

The off-field drama is almost as gripping as the on-field struggles for the Carolina Panthers.
