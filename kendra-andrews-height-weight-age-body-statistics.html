Kendra Andrews is an American dancer and actress, best known for her movie roles in Farewell Colette (2001) as Dancer, Step Up 3D (2010) as Anala, Monster Heroes (2010) as Carmen, and Footloose (2011) as Cowboys’ Dancer. After a 3-year break, she made her return with the role of Amber in a comedy movie Bachelors (2015), followed by roles of Rachel Molina in Blindspotting (2018), and Meghan in Killer Contractor (2019). Kendra also had 1-episode roles in TV series House (2011) as Dina and Masters of S*x (2016) as Bunny.

Kendra Andrews Facts

  • When she was 2 years old, her family moved to San Antonio, Texas. Only a year and a half later, the family moved to Valrico, Florida, when her dad got a job as an investigative reporter for NBC-affiliated station, WFLA-TV.
  • She has joked that her hairstyle as a kid resembled that of Jim Carrey.
  • Many people told Kendra that her mom, during her teenage years, looked almost identical to her.
  • She is a big dog lover and especially adores her sister’s Golden Retriever, Howard. Furthermore, her dad Steve also temporarily takes care of dogs at home to help out a local Southeastern Guide Dogs organization.
  • At the age of 20, she and her friend, Janet Claire, had a near-fatal car accident. As a result, she suffered from PTSD for years but later overcame it. Two women reunited more than a decade later in July 2018.
  • Kendra is very empathetic. In October 2018, she talked about the 2-year journey she took with a teenager who had trouble moving her limbs. To help her, Kendra used movement therapy in addition to the sound of their mutual favorite songs.
