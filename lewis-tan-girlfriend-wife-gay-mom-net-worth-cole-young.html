Lewis Tan Mom

Lewis Tan is half-Chinese and half-British. He was born in Salford, England, and was raised mostly in Los Angeles. Growing up, he also lived in France, Thailand, Spain, and China.

His mom, Joanne Cassidy, is a retired British fashion model from the United Kingdom. Concerning ethnicity, she is either Irish or British. While his dad, Philip Tan, is a martial artist-actor, and stunt coordinator of Chinese origin from Singapore.
