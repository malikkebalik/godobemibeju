Biography

Travis Tritt was born in Marietta, GA on February 9, 1963. Country singer whose debut album was the 1989 release Country Club. He won a Grammy Award for the duet “The Whiskey Ain’t Workin’” with Marty Stuart. He was a prominent member of Grand Ole Opry, along with Minnie Pearl. He worked at a furniture store and as a supermarket clerk during his teen years. He recorded his first demos with Warner Bros. Records.
