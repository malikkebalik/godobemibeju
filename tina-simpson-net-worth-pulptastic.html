What Is Tina Simpson’s Net Worth?

Tina Simpson, American entrepreneur and mother of singers Ashlee Simpson and Jessica Simpson, has a net worth of $20 million. She co-founded The Jessica Simpson Collection in 2005, which reached $1 billion in sales after a decade.

Tina has appeared on various TV shows, including “Newlyweds: Nick & Jessica” (2003–2005), “The Ashlee Simpson Show” (2004–2005), and “Ashlee+Evan” (2018).

Early Life of Tina Simpson

Tina Ann Drew, born on January 18, 1960, in Waco, Texas, is the daughter of Dorothy Jean and Acy Edward Drew Jr. She grew up with her sister Connie.

Jessica Simpson and Tina Simpson Regain Ownership of Jessica Simpson Collection

In 1999, the Simpson family moved to Los Angeles to support Jessica’s career. Tina became Jessica’s stylist, and they launched The Jessica Simpson Collection in 2005. The company has since expanded into clothing, accessories, swimwear, and home decor, and was carried by 650 department stores within five years.

Sequential Brands Group Inc. became a majority shareholder in 2015, but filed for bankruptcy in August 2021. As of October 2021, Tina and Jessica have complete ownership of the company, after paying $65 million for Sequential’s stake. Jessica is now CEO, and Tina is president. The duo plans to expand the brand into health and wellness, skincare, furniture, and more.

Tina Simpson’s Personal Life

Tina married Joe Simpson on August 19, 1978, and had two daughters, Jessica and Ashlee, before divorcing in 2013. Joe was a youth pastor at the time of the wedding and later became Jessica and Ashlee’s manager. Rumors of Joe’s alleged involvement with a 21-year-old model emerged during the divorce, but the family denied any third party involvement.

In June 2014, Tina got engaged to Jon Goldstein, the owner of Johnny Appleseed Landscaping Company, after two years of dating. Tina is a proud grandmother of six, including Bronx Mowgli Wentz, Jagger Snow Ross, Ziggy Blu Ross, Maxwell Drew Johnson, Ace Knute Johnson, and Birdie Mae Johnson.
