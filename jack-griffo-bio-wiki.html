Who is Jack Griffo?

Jack Griffo is an American actor as well as a singer. He is best known for his role as Max Thunderman on the Nickelodeon series The Thundermans. He released his hit single “Drew Tube” in 2013.

Jack Griffo: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity

Rising artist Jack Griffo was born and raised in Orlando, Florida on 11 December 1996.

His birth name is Jack Davis Griffo. He has four more siblings with whom he was raised. He is of mixed(English and Italian) ethnicity and has an American nationality.

Jack Griffo: Professional Life, Career

Talking about their career journey Jack Griffo, started his career journey in the entertainment industry in 2011 and he is still active in it. He made his acting debut by starring in the role of Benny in an American martial arts-inspired comedy television series Kickin’ It.

He kept on showing up in the Disney and Nickelodeon arrangement like “Jessie” in 2011, ‘Marvin’ in 2012, and ‘See me Dad’ in 2012. These appearances helped him to his lead part in Nickelodeon’s The Thunderman. He first won a nomination for the Kids Choice Awards for Favourite TV Actor in 2014.
