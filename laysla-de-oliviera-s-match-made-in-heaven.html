Laysla De Oliviera's Match Made in Heaven!

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Not many manage to create a remarkable achievement like Laysla De Oliveira in the glamorous television industry. The gorgeous star is famous from the recent Netflix’s In the Tall Grass.

Almost everyone who follows the entertainment industry today knows Laysla, but what about her personal life? So, today, we bring you all the needed details about her secrets and relationships. 

Match made from Work with Jonathan Keltz

Maybe it was the work of destiny since the Netflix actress currently shares a romantic relationship with fellow HBO actor, Jonathan Keltz. The lovely duo is together since 2013, and there are no signs of separating them yet.  

The celebrity duo crossed paths for the first time during a production of a movie in 2013. Slowly getting used to each other, they dated from Valentine’s day 2014.

The adorable couple also acted together in Acquainted back in July 2019. Similarly, the widely gossiped duo also attended the movie premiere of Disney’s Aladdin.

The charming artist calls her renowned boyfriend with a nickname, Avocado and also her doggy daddy.

A Little Dog-Family

Oliviera and her soulmate, Keltz seem to create a little promo for their future ahead, as they adopted a cute puppy sometime ahead. Both of them are passionate pet lovers.

The celebrity couple considers each other as the parents to Lua De Keltz, the name of their dog. Quick Fact: Oliviera’s dog has her own Instagram channel.

Starred In The Tall Grass

The 27-year-old recently landed a breakthrough role on the billion dollars-worth network, Netflix’s In the Tall Grass. She is currently in hear peak age of career with numerous offers and demands.

Veronica at Guest of Honour

Continuing the good run, Oliviera appeared in the Canadian movie, Guest of Honour. She played the lead role of Veronica in the thrilling drama.
