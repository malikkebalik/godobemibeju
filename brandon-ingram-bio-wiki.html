Who is Brandon Ingram?

Brandon Ingram is an American well-known professional basketball player.

He is best known as the player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

He plays from a small forward position wearing Jersey number 14.

Brandon Ingram: Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity, Nationality

Brandon Ingram was born in Kinston, Texas. He was born on 2 September 1997. He is 25 years old as of 2023.

Ingram is of African American ethnicity and has American nationality.

Named Brandon Xavier Ingram. He is the son of Donald (father) and Joann Ingram (mother).

He has two half-siblings, one elder brother Donovan and another elder sister Brittany.

His father was a police officer but now he is full-time at a welding plant where he makes forklifts.

Since childhood, he has built up a strong interest in basketball games.

Brandon Ingram: Education, School/College University

Ingram studied at Kinston High School where he played basketball and was named MVP of the state championship game after scoring 28 points, 10 rebounds, and 5 blocks in a winning match against East Lincoln High School.

Later, he attended Duke University where he received his first ACC Rookie of the Week honor.

Brandon Ingram: Professional Life and Career

Brandon Ingram started playing professional basketball in 2016 and he is still active in it.

He started playing basketball professionally after getting selected by the Los Angeles Lakers in the 2016 NBA draft.

He became the second-youngest player drafted in 2016.

On 23 August 2016, he signed a rookie contract with the Lakers and started playing for the Lakers.

In his debut game on 26 October 2016, he recorded 9 points in a winning match against Houston Rockets.

He participated in the Rising Stars Challenge along with his teammate D’Angelo Russell during NBA All-Star Weekend.

He was also selected to the 2016 national basketball selecting team of the United States which trained with the 2016 US Olympic Basketball Team.

On 26 February 2017, he recorded his career-high of 22 points in a match against the San Antonio Spurs.

Till now Ingram hasn’t been traded to any other teams and he is still playing for Los Angeles Lakers.
