Tee Higgins, Gabe Davis or Christian Kirk? Pick 2

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Q: Pick 1 for flex in a PPR league. — Chandler M.

Given your decision, I’d say we need to recognize how well you did in your draft. This is a tough decision, as Dillon looks like one of this year’s true values. Somehow you ended up with one of the few guys I’d start in front of him in Patterson.

The thing about C-Pat is he’s game-script independent right now because of his diverse usage. Make no mistake, he is not being used a scat back or some gadget player; he is the engine of the offense and the best player on the field when he carries the ball.

His Week 1 line was absurd: 22 attempts, 120 rushing yards, one rushing TD, 19 routes, five targets, three receptions, 16 percent team targets, 26 percent target per route, 5.4 scrimmage yards per touch.

Ahead or behind, the offense goes through Patterson.
