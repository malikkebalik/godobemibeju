Overall Meaning

In "Bodak Yellow," Cardi B is not afraid to declare her success and boast about her wealth. She confidently asserts that no one can compete with her, stating "Said I'm the shit, couldn't fuck with me if they wanted to." She makes it clear that she makes "money moves" rather than wasting time dancing or partying. She also emphasizes the value of her designer shoes by repeating, "These expensive, these is red bottoms, these is bloody shoes," and notes that she can afford to buy multiple pairs at once. Cardi B references her past as a stripper when she says, "My pussy feel like a lake, he wanna swim with his face." Meanwhile, she also takes shots at some of her haters, saying that if she sees someone and doesn't speak, it means she doesn't like them. The song finishes with a short verse where Cardi B suggests that she has even more money coming to her.
