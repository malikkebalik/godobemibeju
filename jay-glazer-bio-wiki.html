Who is Jay Glazer?

Jay Glazer is an American sportswriter.

Also, he is an insider of the National Football League for Fox Sports.

Furthermore, he is also an entrepreneur.

Glazer is the co-founder of MMAthlethics which is a Las Vegas and Los Angeles-based NFL athlete martial arts training center.

Jay Glazer: Age, Parents, Siblings, Childhood, Ethnicity

Jay Glazer was born on December 26, 1969, in Manalapan Township, New Jersey, USA.

As of 2023, his age is 54.

He was born to parents Edward A. Glazer(father) and Irene Glazer(mother).

His actual birth name is Jason Charles Glazer. Glazer spent his childhood days in New Jersey.

His ethnicity is Ashkenazi Jewish.

Education: School/College, University

Talking about his educational background, he attended Pace University in New York.

From there, he graduated with a mass media degree.

Jay Glazer: Professional Life, Career

Jay Glazer started his career as a reporter for the New York Post where he was very active and was admired for his reporting.

He was then able to establish a connection to football players around the USA.

He was then successful to work as the National Football League insider for Fox Sports in 2004.

In January 2018, he joined Paramount Network and Bellator MMA to be included in their broadcast team.

He also serves as a Host for Bellator’s MMA fights on the Paramount Network.

Furthermore, he has also written several articles for the Giants Newsweekly.

He also trains the NFL players in Mixed Martial Arts during the off-season.

Aside from being a journalist, Glazer is also featured in some movies over the years.

Some of the movies in which he appeared include ‘The Longest Yard, ‘The Game Plan’, and ‘The League’.

Moreover, he also appeared as himself in Ballers (2015) and Bones (2017).
