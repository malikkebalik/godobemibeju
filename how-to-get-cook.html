How to get &amp; cook

Publish date: 2024-09-02

If you want to get into cooking in Palia and want to get your hands on the Creamy Carrot Soup recipe, this guide will tell you where you can obtain it, and how to cook it. It’s relatively easy to get your hands on this recipe, and it is a simple recipe to cook. Here is how you can cook Creamy Carrot Soup in the game.

How to get the Creamy Carrot Soup recipe in Palia

In order to obtain the recipe so that you can make the Creamy Carrot Soup, you have to complete a certain quest first. This quest is called “Manners, Manners”. To begin this quest, go speak to the Librarian, Caleri. Once your quest has begun, you will have to read a book titled “The Majiri’s Book of Etiquette and Manual of Politeness” book that you can find on Caleri’s desk. She will ask what you learned from the book, and the correct option to choose is “To say please and thank you?”

Now that you have your very own Library Card, you will be able to get the recipe. Head to the right, and there will be a shelf next to the window. Press F to read the book and you’ll be able to get your Creamy Carrot Soup Recipe.

How to make Creamy Carrot Soup in Palia

To make Creamy Carrot Soup, you need four ingredients. They are the following:

  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Spice Sprout
  • 1 Milk
  • 1 Wild Garlic

Now that you have all of your ingredients, take them to your cooking station, where you will need to prep the ingredients and chop the Wild Garlic. It will take you a minute to cook. It will give you 125 Focus, but you also have the option to sell it for 41 gold, with a higher quality soup selling for 61 gold.

That’s everything you need to know about how to make Creamy Carrot Soup in Palia.
