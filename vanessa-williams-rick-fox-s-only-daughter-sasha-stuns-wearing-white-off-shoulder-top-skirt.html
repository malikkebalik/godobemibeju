Vanessa Williams &amp; Rick Fox's Only Daughter Sasha Stuns Wearing White off-Shoulder Top &amp; Skirt

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Sasha Gabriella Fox, the daughter of Vanessa Williams and her ex-husband Rick Fox, stunned in a white dress in a recent picture shared on Instagram.

Vanessa Williams and Rick Fox's daughter Sasha Gabriella Fox made a stunning appearance on Instagram when she posted a picture on her Instagram Story showing her posing with two of her friends.

One of those friends was Tori Partika who celebrated her 21st birthday on Wednesday. Sasha wished Partika a happy birthday, praying that her new year is everything she imagined.

Vanessa Williams and her ex-husband Rick Fox at the film premiere of her movie, "Light It Up" in 1999 | Photo: Getty Images

The picture Sasha shared was from a post Partika made in August where she shared several photos of her and her friends while they were on a summer vacation in Italy.

In the snapshot, Sasha posed with Partika and another one of her friends, Juliette Benedetto. Sasha wore a white off-shoulder top which she matched with a long, white skirt. In the caption to the post, Partika reminisced on her Italian experience, writing:

"Wishin I was back here feelin [sic] this Italian summer lovin [sic]?."

Sasha has grown remarkably and has changed so much from the shy little 7-year-old she was when she joined her parents to attend the Camp Ronald McDonald For GoodTimes 15th Annual Family Halloween Carnival in 2017.

Sasha is 20 years old now and is a model and actress by profession. In November 2019, she made her film debut when she starred in the film "One Fine Christmas." She and her dad appeared on Extra TV to promote the movie.

Sasha also appeared as a fit model on air during her mother's first live HSN appearance.

Sasha Gabriella Fox poses with her friends, Tori Partika and Juliette Benedetto to celebrate Tori's birthday | Photo: Instagram/sashafoxyy

During the interview, Sasha revealed she always had the desire to act, but her parents eased her into it as they wanted her to have a nice high school experience, for which she is thankful.

Sasha graduated from The Hervey High School and is studying film production at Chapman University's Dodge College of Film & Arts. She is signed to two model talent agencies, Natural Models LA and One Management.

As an actress and a model, Sasha will learn so much from her mom, who is known for her role as Wilhelmina Slater, the vicious creative director in the comedy-drama series, "Ugly Betty."

Williams is also the first Black Miss America and recently showed off her incredible sense of fashion with the release of a clothing line with HSN last year.

She made the clothing line's launch a family affair by enrolling her mother, Helen Williams, and her daughter, Melanie, to model the line in campaign photos. Sasha also appeared as a fit model on air during her mother's first live HSN appearance.

Williams has been married three times. Her first marriage was to Ramon Hervey II, who she was married to from 1987-1997. She and Fox were married from 1999 to 2005, while she married her current husband, Jim Skrip in 2015.

Williams has four children, Melanie, singer Jillian, and Devin, who she shares with Hervey, and Sasha. She has gained experience from all of her marriages and can be trusted for relationship advice.

In a Glamour article, Williams noted that all the men she has married are good and brought joy to her in different ways before advising people to be clear about who they are marrying before doing so.
