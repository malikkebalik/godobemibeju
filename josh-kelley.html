Josh Kelley’s Childhood, Biography, and Education

Josh Kelley was born in Augusta, Georgia on January 30, 1980, to Gayle (mother) and Dr. John W. Kelley (father). He is of American descent and of North American ancestry. Kelley enrolled in Lakeside High School and graduated from there in 1998. He later attended the University of Mississippi in Oxford, Mississippi.

Josh Kelley’s Professional Career

Josh Kelley began his singing career at the age of 11 when he formed a band with his brother, Charles Kelley. Inside Blue is the name of their band. Changing Faces, his first independent album, was released in 2001.
