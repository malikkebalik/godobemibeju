Rosa Acosta Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Who is Rosa Acosta?

Rosa Acosta is a Dominican actress and model. She loves ballet dancing and fitness. She is most famous for her role in Straight from the Horse Mouth, Walter Latham’s Comedy After Dark, and Bird.

She is the CEO/Founder of her clothing brand: cossamia, Body by Rosa Acosta.

Rosa Acosta: Age, Parents, Siblings

Rosa Acosta was born on April 30, 1984, in Santiago de los Caballeros, Dominic Republic. She is 37 years old.

And as of now, the identity of her parents is currently not available. But we know that she has one brother DJ Smoke.

Talking about her childhood, she has struggled most of her childhood to improve her career. She was raised by her grandparents who were not modern in culture and thinking. So, she was not too free to enjoy her childhood.

In which school did Rosa Acosta attend?

Since Rosa did not experience a stable and happy childhood, she was not able to attend school on a regular basis.

She graduated with a Bachelor in Art with a mention to Classical Ballet from Norma Garcia Ballet School. In addition, she attended the Institute of Culture and Arts.

Rosa Acosta: Professional Life, Career

Rosa Acosta initially started her professional career by featuring in a music video.

This video made her widely famous and later landed her in several music videos of the artists including, Chris Brown, Lil’ Wayne, Drake, and Diddy.

Some of her notable music videos are, Back to the Crib, Best I Ever Had, I Can Transform Ya, Must Be Money, and many others.

Her fame grew exponentially after that. As a result, she was featured in fashion magazines such as XXL, King, Blackmen, and Smooth.

During Rosa’s professional career she also modeled for Colt 45, Phat Phruit, and Presidente. Rosa has also appeared in the movies, Still a Teen, Bird, Straight from the Horses Mouth, and Walter Latham’s Comedy After Dark.

In addition, she was seen on the commercial of brands including, Entourage, Frito Lays, Brilliante Detergent, and Orange Dominicana.

And, she is still continuing her career which has been successful so far. She has also started her own YouTube channel in which she uploads fitness videos.

And recently, she has launched her own perfume brand. In addition, she has her own clothing and fitness brand under the name CossaMia.

Awards

Rosa has had a successful modeling career than her other professional practices. In 2009, she was titled with XXL magazine’s Eye Candy of The Year. In addition, she received the Urban Model Awards’ Model of the Year in 2010.
