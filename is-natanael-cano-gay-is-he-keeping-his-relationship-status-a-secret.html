Natanael Cano Career

Natanael Cano started his career by putting on YouTube guitar covers and original songs. Rancho Humilde, a record label based in Los Angeles that focuses on urban regional Mexican music, heard about him and signed him. In 2019, he signed with them, and his first record, “Todo Es Diferente,” came out. The album quickly became famous in Mexico and the US, especially with younger people.

“Soy El Diablo,” the first song from the album, went viral on social media sites like TikTok. This helped Cano become a rising star in the Regional Mexican music scene. Cano kept putting out new songs after the success of “Todo Es Diferente.” One of these was the album “Corridos Tumbados, Vol. 1,” which featured collaborations with Bad Bunny and Ovi.

The record was well-reviewed and sold well. It went to the top of the Billboard Latin record charts and got Cano nominated for several awards. Cano’s third record, “Trap Tumbado,” solidified him as one of the best artists in the “corridos tumbados” subgenre.
